Neurological Sciences Department

Expert care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatment.

Brain Monitoring

Continuous brain activity monitoring to diagnose neurological conditions and assess brain function accurately.

EEG Testing

Electroencephalogram (EEG) testing to evaluate electrical activity in the brain and detect neurological abnormalities.

Neurological Exam

Comprehensive neurological examinations to assess reflexes, motor skills, sensory function, and cognitive health.

Treatment Plans

Personalized treatment plans designed to manage neurological conditions effectively and improve patient quality of life.

Surgical Services Department

Our surgical department delivers safe, precise, and minimally invasive procedures performed by highly experienced surgeons.

Minimally Invasive

We specialize in minimally invasive procedures that reduce pain, minimize scarring, and promote quicker healing with shorter hospital stays.

Advanced Procedures

Our department performs a wide range of advanced surgical procedures using state-of-the-art technology and evidence-based practices.

Safe Operations

Patient safety is our top priority, with strict protocols, advanced monitoring, and experienced surgical teams ensuring every procedure is performed with care.

Recovery Support

We provide complete post-surgical recovery support, including pain management, rehabilitation guidance, and continuous follow-up care.

Dental Care Department

Our Dental Care Department offers comprehensive oral healthcare for patients of all ages. With modern equipment and experienced dental specialists, we focus on maintaining healthy smiles through preventive, restorative, and cosmetic treatments.

Oral Health

We provide complete oral health assessments and treatments to prevent dental problems and ensure long-term gum and tooth health.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional teeth cleaning services help remove plaque, prevent cavities, and maintain fresh breath for a confident smile.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhance your smile with advanced cosmetic dental solutions, including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile correction treatments.

Orthodontics

Our orthodontic services correct misaligned teeth and bite issues using modern braces and clear aligners for all age groups.

Ophthalmology Department

Our Ophthalmology Department provides comprehensive eye care services using advanced diagnostic technology and modern treatment methods. Our experienced eye specialists are committed to protecting and improving your vision at every stage of life.

Vision Testing

Accurate and detailed vision examinations to detect eye conditions early and ensure optimal visual clarity for daily activities.

Retinal Imaging

Advanced retinal imaging services help diagnose and monitor retinal disorders with high-precision imaging techniques.

Laser Surgery

We offer safe and effective laser eye treatments to correct vision problems and manage various eye conditions with minimal recovery time.

Eye Care Plans

Personalized eye care plans designed to maintain long-term eye health through regular checkups, preventive care, and timely treatments.

Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department offers comprehensive heart care services focused on accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and long-term heart health management. Our expert cardiologists use advanced technology to deliver safe and reliable cardiac care.

Heart Monitoring

Continuous and non-invasive heart monitoring services to detect irregularities early and ensure timely medical intervention.

ECG Analysis

Advanced ECG testing and interpretation to assess heart rhythm, electrical activity, and overall cardiac function accurately.

Blood Tests

Comprehensive cardiac-related blood tests to identify risk factors, monitor heart conditions, and support precise diagnosis.

Preventive Care

Personalized preventive care programs designed to reduce heart disease risk through lifestyle guidance, regular screenings, and early intervention.
